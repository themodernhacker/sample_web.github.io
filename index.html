Bio

My Bio

Hello Boys and Girls,

I wanna talk to you directly, I had the best day when I thought to quit NEET preparations and follow my passion as a Cyber Security Student.

I know, I know My family member was frustrated with me, But I think they have to because I lost my father’s and Uncle’s dream, They always saw me as a doctor.

We belong to a middle-class family in India. At that time my decision was like brain freezing for everyone because they spent more than 1.5 lakh rupees on a year in my studies. and if you don’t know then I will tell you that this amount is much more than my father’s annual income, that’s why everyone got shocked. My father has a small welding workshop in our village, earning money to give me and my brother a good education in private school is very difficult for him, that’s why my respected uncle and grandfather we all live in the same house, they helped us.

Shortly, when I crossed high school and dropped my plan, I have only one thing in my mind, every difficulty is temporary, but if today I follow my passion, it will give me long-term success and happiness to my family.

At that point, I decided to follow my passion and then started doing some certifications in OS, Hacking, Networking, and tried to gather knowledge as much as I can. Then I took my life-changing decision to apply to some universities to pursue my bachelor’s in Computer Science and additional Cyber Security in the USA.

I got an offer letter from one of the best universities in California for my bachelor’s and applied for a scholarship. My dedication towards my passion changed my family member’s mindset. After seeing this, they encourage me to study in the USA, for this all of my family members also supported me and that is the happiest moment for me nowadays.

Recently if I talk about myself and what I’m doing now at this time, then I’m preparing for my visa interview as I’m going to attend my university in fall 2022.

I will share my full journey in my blog section, but for now, just remember one thing:-

"If you follow your dreams and passion today, It will automatically give you

success and happiness in a long term".
